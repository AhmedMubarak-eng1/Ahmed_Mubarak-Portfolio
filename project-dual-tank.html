What was delivered

  • Implemented a dual-tank control strategy with pumps and valves coordinated through reusable logic structure.
  • Applied alternation logic, maintenance behaviour, and operator-facing HMI status/control concepts to make the system easier to manage and support.
  • Used the project to show control architecture quality, not just point-to-point logic.
