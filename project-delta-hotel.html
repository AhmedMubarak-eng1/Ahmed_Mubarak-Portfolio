Engineering story

The Delta Ottawa compressor project is the kind of work that immediately raises the quality of your portfolio because it combines controls, electrical design, commissioning, and practical building operations in one case study.

2Compressors coordinated
FullDesign to startup scope
HighOperations impact

What was delivered

  • Owned the control concept, electrical design intent, panel architecture, operating strategy, and field commissioning approach for a dual-compressor BAS.
  • Built logic for automatic alternation, runtime balancing, alarm handling, pressure-based operation, and abnormal-condition detection including leak-related behaviour.
  • Coordinated with hardware suppliers and project stakeholders to move the system from engineering concept toward a real installed asset.
  • Supported certification-related readiness, startup verification, and final handover with a focus on reliability and operator confidence.

Why this project matters

  • Shows you can do much more than PLC programming — it proves design ownership, practical execution, and strong engineering communication.
  • Reads as client-facing work, which is powerful for employers looking for engineers who can be trusted with real scope and responsibility.
  • Bridges engineering and product/project thinking because it required deciding what the system should do, how it should fail safely, and how operators would actually use it.
