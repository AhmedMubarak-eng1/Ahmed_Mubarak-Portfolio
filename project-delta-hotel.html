Building Automation · Capstone

Delta (Marriott) Ottawa Hotel – Dual Air Compressor BAS

Midwest Services Canada Jan – March 2025

Overview

Led the end-to-end design and implementation of a dual air compressor building automation system for the Delta Ottawa Hotel (Marriott brand). Responsible for the full project lifecycle — from requirements definition and supplier coordination through to on-site commissioning, operator training, and ESA/ESAFE certification. This was a capstone-level project delivered while working part-time at Midwest Services Canada.

What Was Built

  • Designed full system architecture, electrical schematics, power distribution, control circuits, and protection schemes in AutoCAD Electrical
  • Programmed a Siemens LOGO! PLC for automatic and manual operation modes, load balancing between two compressors, runtime tracking, and preventive maintenance scheduling
  • Integrated advanced alarm handling — leak detection alerts, low pressure warnings, and abnormal operation flags sent directly to engineering staff
  • Automated daily engineer reports providing real-time visibility of system health, runtime hours, and upcoming maintenance milestones
  • Designed and built a CSA-compliant custom control panel including power distribution, protection devices, and all I/O wiring
  • Managed supplier coordination and procurement of all panel components and instrumentation
  • Commissioned the system on-site at the Delta Ottawa Hotel and trained hotel engineering staff on operation and maintenance procedures
  • Achieved ESA/ESAFE certification — fully compliant with Ontario electrical safety standards

Results & Impact

Delivered a fully certified, standards-compliant solution that significantly reduced unplanned downtime, extended compressor service cycles, and gave hotel engineering staff complete real-time visibility over system health — preventing costly reactive maintenance. The client received a complete documentation package including wiring drawings, logic descriptions, and maintenance procedures for long-term support.
