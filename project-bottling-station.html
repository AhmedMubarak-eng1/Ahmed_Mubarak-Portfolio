What was delivered

  • Installed, wired, and programmed a bottling station with pneumatic actuators, operator screens, and sequence-driven process logic.
  • Mapped and tested I/O devices, verified safety interlocks, and supported reliable station behaviour across manual and automatic modes.
  • Built useful experience in another major PLC platform while reinforcing HMI design and machine-level troubleshooting skills.

Why this project matters

  • This project is great for demonstrating you are not locked into one vendor or one workflow.
  • It also shows machine control fundamentals clearly: sequence, sensors, interlocks, actuators, and operator interaction.
  • When presented well, it helps communicate adaptability and solid controls fundamentals.
